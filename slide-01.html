Instructor Information

Facilator Notes

  • 9.2.3.6* Wall-mounted sidewall sprinklers shall be restrained to prevent movement.
  • This requirement is especially critical for CPVC piping systems. The piping and/or the fittings need to be restrained to prevent movement of the sidewall sprinkler. Activation of the sidewall sprinkler can have a great reactive force which can move the sprinkler out of position, or possibly cause damage to the piping.
  • Ask the students, what is one of the best ways to restrain wall mounted sidewall sprinklers from movement? Have them share their answers in class, then share the suggested answer below.
  • Suggested answer: To prevent unwanted movement of the wall mounted sidewall sprinkler, a hanger that provides restraint should be installed on the piping or fitting, as close to the sidewall sprinkler as possible.

Questions and Answers

  • Ask the students: What is the maximum allowable distance between hangers or supports for CPVC piping systems? What information is needed to determine the maximum allowable distance? Where would they find this information?
  • Have them discuss their answers in class. After iscussion, advance to the next slide for answers.
