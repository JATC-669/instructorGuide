Course Timing

The total amount of time needed to instruct this course is 4 hours (240 Minutes).

As the facilitator, you are responsible for determining the pace ofthe course. All course content must be communicated, but flexibility in instruction and timing is based on your assessment of the learner's needs.

This course was written based on requirements from the following editions of NFPA Standards:

  • 2013 NFPA 13
  • 2013 NFPA 20
  • 2014 NFPA 25

Specific local requirements may be different depending on edition of the standard enforced and other AHJ requirements.
